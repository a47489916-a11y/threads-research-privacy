Privacy Policy — Threads Research Tool

Last Updated: April 1, 2026  |  Effective Date: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Threads Research Tool ("the App", "we", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information obtained through the Threads API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.

The App is a research tool designed to analyze publicly available posts on the Threads platform. Its purpose is to study trends and patterns in public discourse, with a focus on understanding how harmful content — such as fraudulent job postings and deceptive recruitment schemes — spreads in online communities, contributing to a safer online environment.

2. Data We Collect

The App accesses the following types of data through the Threads API:

2-A. Public Post Data

  • Post content (text of publicly visible Threads posts)
  • Post metadata (post ID, timestamp, permalink)
  • Media type (text, image, video, carousel — metadata only, not media files)

2-B. Public Profile Data

  • Username (Threads handle, e.g., @username)
  • User ID (unique numeric identifier assigned by Threads)
  • Profile name (display name)

2-C. Engagement Data

  • Reply content (text of public replies to relevant posts)
  • Reply metadata (reply ID, timestamp, author username/ID)

2-D. Search Data

  • Keyword search results (posts matching specific search queries via Threads API keyword search functionality)

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Private messages or direct messages
  • Non-public posts or content from private accounts
  • Passwords or authentication credentials of third parties
  • Financial information, health data, or precise location of users
  • Contact lists, browsing history, or device information of Threads users
  • Media files (images, videos) — only metadata is processed

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We process the data described above solely for the following purposes:

  1. Academic and Social Research: Studying publicly available content to understand trends in harmful online activities, such as fraudulent job postings and deceptive recruitment schemes targeting vulnerable individuals
  2. Pattern Analysis: Analyzing patterns and trends in publicly available posts to contribute to a better understanding of online safety challenges
  3. Research Publications: Generating aggregated and anonymized insights for research reports and publications aimed at promoting a safer online environment

We do not use collected data for:

  • Advertising, marketing, or commercial purposes
  • User profiling beyond the scope of the research described above
  • Sale or licensing of data to third parties
  • Training of machine learning models for commercial use
  • Tracking or monitoring of specific individuals

4. Data Storage and Security

Storage

  • Collected data is stored in a local database on secured, access-controlled systems
  • Data is not stored on public cloud services or shared hosting environments
  • Database access is restricted to authorized research personnel only

Security Measures

  • Access tokens and API keys are stored as environment variables, never in source code
  • All API communications use HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Database files are stored on encrypted storage volumes
  • Regular review and deletion of data that is no longer necessary for research purposes

5. Data Retention

  • Active research data: Retained for the duration of the research activity, up to a maximum of 12 months from the date of collection
  • Analyzed and reported data: Summary reports (anonymized/aggregated) may be retained indefinitely; raw data is deleted after 12 months
  • User account deletion: If a Threads user deletes their account or post, we will remove corresponding data from our systems within 30 days of becoming aware of the deletion, or upon notification from Meta
  • Upon request by Meta: Data will be deleted without undue delay upon request from Meta Platforms, Inc.

6. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

  • We do not sell, rent, or license collected data to any third party
  • We do not share raw data with any third party for commercial purposes
  • Aggregated, anonymized findings may be shared in research publications or reports aimed at improving online safety
  • If required by applicable law, data may be disclosed to relevant authorities in compliance with valid legal processes

7. Legal Basis for Processing

Data processing is conducted on the basis of:

  • Legitimate interest in academic and social research aimed at understanding and improving online safety
  • Compliance with applicable laws where required

All data processed is publicly available information posted by users on the Threads platform with the intent of public visibility.

8. User Rights

If you are a Threads user and your publicly available post has been collected by our system, you have the right to:

  • Request information about whether your data has been collected
  • Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from or about children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). If we become aware that data of a child has been collected, we will delete it promptly.
